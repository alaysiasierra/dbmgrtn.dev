YOU'RE NOW HEARING THE FUTURE

KBeach Radio 88.1 FM HD-3

KBeach Radio is a student-run-and-programmed, state-of-the art radio station that currently broadcasts on the Internet and Hybrid Digital (OFDM Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiple Access) radio.The station has been primarily a space for music appreciation and as an optional use, a means to gain practical experience in various music industry functions. Working with KBeach is consistently popular among students and attracts a great cross-section of the CSULB population as well as volunteers from the surrounding community. You never know what our content will be when you tune in to KBeach, but you can rely on the fact that it will be unique, cool, and student-driven. KBeach always encourages the student voice of CSULB and protects the freedom of speech of all within the guidelines of the FCC. Many of our programs are intellectually or opinion-based in conjunction with many gifted professors, our award-winning debate team and journalism department. Our News Department is always looking for important stories and features to bring to you. And as always, we'll continue to bring you up-to-date music when our active students aren't bringing you live content from CSULB and beyond.
